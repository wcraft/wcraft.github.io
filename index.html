About Me

I am an investigative data reporter, specializing in government accountability. I use public records, data analysis, and in-depth reporting to cover criminal justice, the environment, and the way our institutions function. I worked with APM Reports, a team of public radio reporters from 2015 to 2022. My work has appeared on NPR, Reveal from the Center for Investigative Reporting, public radio stations around the country, and the podcasts In The Dark and Sent Away. It has been honored with two Peabodies, a DuPont, and a George Polk award. More importantly, it has spurred policy changes that have made a difference in people’s lives.
